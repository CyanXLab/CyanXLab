试卷结构：
- I. Listening Comprehension（第1-10题,每题1分；第11-20题，每题1.5分；共25分）
- II. Grammar and Vocabulary（每题1分；共20分）
- III. Reading Comprehension
- IV. Writing（含概要写作/作文）

At the end of each conversation, a question will be asked about what was said...
